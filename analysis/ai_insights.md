## Summary of Data Findings

Analysis of the operational workflow dataset revealed that attended appointments accounted for the majority of visits, though no-shows represented a consistent portion across service lines. Average wait times varied by department, with some departments experiencing longer waits than others. Communication channels also differed in their association with appointment attendance, suggesting opportunities for targeted outreach.

- Use data-driven feedback loops to support continuous workflow improvement across service lines.
